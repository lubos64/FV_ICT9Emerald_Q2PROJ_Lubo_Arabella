What is French Food?

Rich flavors, a variety of regional specialties, and cooking methods that have impacted cookery around the world are all hallmarks of French cuisine. Fresh, premium ingredients like butter, cream, cheese, wine, and herbs are highlighted, resulting in a harmonious blend of sweet and savory flavors. Traditional foods like croissants, escargot, and coq au vin showcase France's rich gastronomic heritage. French cuisine also places a high importance on presentation, with tasteful plating and meticulous attention to detail being essential components of the meal.
